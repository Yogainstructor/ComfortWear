Privacy Policy

At ComfortWear™, we design comfortable, easy-to-wear clothing specifically for seniors and elderly individuals. Protecting the privacy and trust of our customers, caregivers, and website visitors is extremely important to us.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or contact us for senior clothing inquiries.

Who This Website Is For

ComfortWear™ serves:

  • Senior citizens looking for comfortable daily clothing
  • Family members purchasing clothing for aging parents or grandparents
  • Caregivers seeking easy-wear clothing solutions

Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide a better experience:

  • Name and contact details (when you contact us)
  • Email address for customer support communication
  • Basic browsing data such as pages visited and device type

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to:

  • Respond to product questions or support requests
  • Improve senior clothing designs and website usability
  • Ensure secure and smooth website operation

Cookies & Website Analytics

We use cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve navigation, readability, and accessibility for older users.

Cookies do not store sensitive personal information. You can disable cookies anytime from your browser settings.

Third-Party Tools

ComfortWear™ may use trusted third-party services such as analytics or advertising platforms to understand website performance. These services follow their own privacy standards and policies.

Data Protection & Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Your Rights

  • Ask for correction or deletion of your data
